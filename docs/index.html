Hey everyone, it's been a while. A week ago, I released a website called Castletool. It was meant to be a way for every Castle user to import full color images and giant midi files. Even if they were on iPhone or were inexperienced with the terminal. I wanted it to be an easy experience. However, it was in a pretty rough state. As soon as I bought a cloudflare domain, it got DDOSed. I tried my best to fix it, but it just wouldn't work. I spent many sleepless nights trying to fix the issues, but it didn't work. It didn't help me that someone posted about it on castle without asking first, that just stressed me out even more cause of the attention. I don't want people to hate the person that did it, i know they meant well. Either way, I was stuck between a rock and a hard place. My server was getting DDOSed, fans were wondering what was happening, and some people were harassing me cause it wasn't working. I have made the rough decision to permanently shut down the servers. I already spent a month coding the website itself, and trying to secure it against attacks would be too much work. To the people who are mad at me for this decision, I want you to know that this is not my job. I had no obligation to make this app, I just wanted to help people do things that castle limits from people. To the people who were patient with me and believed in the project, I am forever grateful. You have no idea how much it helped me. I'm sorry it's to come to this. I hope you all can understand this decision.

-Goose
